Morgan Stanley - Summer Technology Analyst (June-August 2020)

During my three months, I built a a novelty detector application that would identify abnormal trade alerts in thee Notify App (a system used by thousands of traders). I used a combination of the local outlier factor and isolation forests models to build a dectector that would identify abnormal hours or traders based on the number of alerts received. I also built an interactive dashboard that would update every hour and allow users to view descriptive statistics including the number of alerts types of alerts received.
